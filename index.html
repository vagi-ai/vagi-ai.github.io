VAGI : Video AI for General Intelligence

P Jaswanth Reddy1,2
1IIT Tirupati, 2AutoAlign AI
Coming Soon

VAGI (Video AI for General Intelligence) aims to extend the capabilities of multimodal large language models (MLLMs) beyond conventional tasks such as video summarization and question answering. The focus is on enabling deeper reasoning, tool use, and dynamic strategy adaptation for video understanding, depending on the context, use case, and input. By pushing MLLMs past static interpretation, the project explores pathways toward achieving general video intelligence.
